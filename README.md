# 🛒 E-Commerce Funnel Analysis
**SQL · Google BigQuery · Business Intelligence**

> Analyzed a 5-stage e-commerce conversion funnel for 5,000 users to identify revenue leakage, traffic quality gaps, and actionable growth opportunities.

---

## 📊 Key Results at a Glance

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Users Analyzed | 5,000 |
| Overall Conversion Rate | 16.5% |
| Total Revenue Tracked | $87,975 |
| Average Order Value | $106.51 |
| Biggest Drop-Off Identified | View → Cart (69%) |
| Best Performing Channel | Email (62% cart rate) |

---

## 🔍 What I Did

- Wrote **5 SQL queries** in Google BigQuery to dissect funnel performance end-to-end
- Calculated **stage-by-stage conversion rates** to pinpoint where users drop off
- Segmented funnel performance **by traffic source** (organic, paid ads, email, social)
- Performed **time-to-conversion analysis** to measure purchase journey speed
- Conducted a **revenue impact analysis** to quantify the cost of drop-offs

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations Delivered

1. **Fix the View-to-Cart Drop-Off** — 69% of visitors never add to cart; improving to 40% unlocks $25K+ per cycle
2. **Scale the Email Channel** — Email outperforms all sources at 62% cart rate; replicate its model across channels
3. **Restructure Social Media Strategy** — Social converts at only 7%; reallocate budget to higher-ROI channels
4. **Reduce Checkout Friction** — Abandoned cart automation and guest checkout can recover $5K+ per cycle
